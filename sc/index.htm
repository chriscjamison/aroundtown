About Around Town

Dallas Jamison and RaeJ Johnson are your Co-hosts and Executive Producers of Around Town; a TV talk show that highlights people, places and events around town but NOT limited to the St. Louis and East St. Louis metropolitan area.

The purpose of the show is to highlight entrepreneurs and share human interest stories in addition to showing off the small business men and women in our communities!

Keep watching because you'll never know where we'll be Around Town!
